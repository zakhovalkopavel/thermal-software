### Borosilicate Glass

**4. Dingwell, D.B., Knoche, R., Webb, S.L. (1992)**
- **Title:** "The effect of B₂O₃ on the viscosity of haplogranitic liquids"
- **Journal:** Chemical Geology
- **Volume/Issue:** 95(3-4)
- **Pages:** 229-237
- **Year:** 1992
- **DOI:** 10.1016/0009-2541(92)90012-G
- **Application:** Borosilicate glasses, boron anomaly
- **Key Data:** B₂O₃ concentration effects, R ratio dependency
- **Access:** https://doi.org/10.1016/0009-2541(92)90012-G

**5. NIST Standard Reference Material 717a**
- **Title:** "Certificate of Analysis - Borosilicate Glass Viscosity Standard"
- **Organization:** National Institute of Standards and Technology
- **Year:** Multiple certifications (latest 2015)
- **Application:** Primary viscosity reference standard
- **Key Data:** Certified composition, certified viscosity-temperature data, uncertainties
- **Access:** https://www.nist.gov (search SRM 717a)
- **Certificate:** Available online free

## Access Summary

### Free Access
- NIST SRM 717a Certificate
- DOI links (most journals via institutional access)
- ASTM standards (preview only, purchase for full)

### Library Access Required
- Older journals (1950s-1970s)
- Slag Atlas
- Glass handbooks

### Purchase Required
- ASTM standards (full text)
- Glass handbooks
- SciGlass database

### Recommended Priority
1. **NIST SRM 717a** (free, essential)
2. **Lakatos 1972** (library, soda-lime reference)
3. **Dingwell 1992** (DOI, borosilicate)
4. **Giordano 2008** (DOI, aluminosilicate)
5. **ASTM C965-96** (purchase if doing measurements)
