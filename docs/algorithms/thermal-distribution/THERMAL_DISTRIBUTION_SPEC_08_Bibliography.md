# THERMAL DISTRIBUTION SPEC — Bibliography (EN)

1. Gerald Recktenwald — *Transient, One-Dimensional Heat Conduction in a Convectively Cooled Sphere* (Portland State Univ., 2006).
2. University of Washington — *Transient Conduction* (slab/cylinder/sphere; eigenvalues; volume-average weights 1, 2x, 3x²).
3. Simon Fraser University (M. Bahrami) — *Transient Heat Conduction* (Bi; Lc = V/S).
4. NTHU OCW — *Time-Dependent Conduction (Chapter 5)* (one-term solutions; product-solution idea).
5. Michigan Tech (F. A. Morrison) — *Unsteady State Heat Transfer — Short Cut Solutions (Heisler/Gurney-Lurie)*.
6. Golub & Welsch (1969) — *Calculation of Gauss Quadrature Rules*.
7. Ignace Bogaert (2014) — *Iteration-Free Computation of Gauss-Legendre Quadrature Nodes and Weights* (SIAM JSC).
8. A. G. Ostrogorsky (2008) — *Transient heat conduction in spheres for Fo < 0.3 and finite Bi*, Heat and Mass Transfer 44: 1557–1562.
9. Hollow / multi-layer cylinders — analytical formulations for annuli (representative journal sources; see detailed derivations in classical heat-transfer texts and specialized articles).
10. NPTEL / SlideShare lecture decks on transient conduction (Heisler charts background and usage).
