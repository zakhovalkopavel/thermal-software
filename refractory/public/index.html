Available Modules

🔬

Phase Equilibrium Calculator

Calculate liquid/solid phase distribution, mineralogy, and thermal properties at high temperatures. Includes glass viscosity analysis and refractoriness predictions.

  • ✓ Full thermodynamic calculations
  • ✓ Multi-model refractoriness (EN ISO 1893, ASTM, GOST)
  • ✓ Glass viscosity with ASTM C965 fixed points
  • ✓ Mineral phase identification
Open Calculator
🧪

Polyfractional Blend Optimizer

Design optimal particle size distributions with advanced packing models. Predict shrinkage, density, and save custom mix presets to your library.

  • ✓ Andreasen & Funk-Dinger PSD models
  • ✓ CPM & Furnas packing calculators
  • ✓ Shrinkage prediction (MSC sintering)
  • ✓ Custom mix library with export/import
Open Optimizer
